Backend Processing

Our AI processing is handled by secure backend servers. Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS. We retain processed data only temporarily to provide responses and do not use it for training AI models or other commercial purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement several security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data sent to our backend servers is encrypted in transit
  • Local Storage: Your preferences are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API
  • No Persistent Storage: We don't save your selected text or interactions on our servers permanently
  • ActiveTab Permission: We only access the current webpage you're viewing (not your entire browsing history)
  • Domain-Specific Tracking: Extension state is tracked per domain, providing granular control
