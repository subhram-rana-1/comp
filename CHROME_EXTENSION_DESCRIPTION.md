# ExplainAI - Chrome Extension

Transform your reading experience with ExplainAI, an intelligent Chrome extension powered by advanced AI that enhances vocabulary, comprehension, and multilingual learning in real-time. Whether you're reading in English, Arabic, Spanish, French, Hindi, Chinese, Japanese, or any other language, ExplainAI acts as your personal AI tutor, making complex text accessible and helping you learn effortlessly.

---

## Key Features

### AI-Powered Word Meaning & Vocabulary Enhancement
✓ Double-click any word for instant AI-powered explanations with meanings, examples, and context  
✓ Select passages or sentences for comprehensive vocabulary analysis  
✓ Get detailed word definitions, synonyms, and usage examples tailored to your reading context  
✓ Build your vocabulary naturally as you read any website  
✓ **Word Bookmarking:** Save important words with their meanings to your personal collection

### Intelligent Text Explanation & Summary
✓ Select complex passages for AI-generated explanations and summaries  
✓ Get comprehensive summaries that maintain original meaning while making content accessible  
✓ **Page Summarisation:** One-click AI-powered summary of entire webpages  
✓ Perfect for students, professionals, and anyone dealing with technical or academic content  
✓ Quickly understand long articles, research papers, and documentation

### Word Bookmarking & Vocabulary Management
✓ Save words with AI-generated meanings to your personal bookmark collection  
✓ Access all bookmarked words through the dedicated Bookmarks dialog  
✓ Search and filter your bookmarked words for quick reference  
✓ Sort bookmarks alphabetically, by newest, or by oldest  
✓ View source URLs and timestamps for each bookmarked word  
✓ Delete individual bookmarks or clear your entire collection  
✓ Bookmarks synced across all browser tabs

### Multilingual AI Chat
✓ Chat with AI in your preferred language - Arabic, Spanish, French, Hindi, Chinese, Japanese, German, Portuguese, Italian, Russian, Korean, Turkish, and more  
✓ Ask questions in any language, regardless of the website's content language  
✓ Get AI responses in your native language for better comprehension  
✓ Perfect for non-English speakers reading English websites, or vice versa

### Real-Time Learning & Comprehension
✓ Learn vocabulary in context as you read  
✓ Improve reading comprehension with instant AI-powered explanations  
✓ Get instant word meanings and text summaries without leaving the webpage  
✓ Enhance your understanding of any content, in any language

### Seamless Integration
✓ Works on any website - news sites, academic papers, blogs, research papers, and more  
✓ Non-intrusive design that enhances rather than disrupts your reading  
✓ Simple toggle to enable/disable the extension as needed  
✓ Optimized for performance with minimal impact on browsing speed

## How It Works

1. Enable ExplainAI with a simple toggle
2. Double-click any word for instant AI-powered word meanings and vocabulary explanations
3. Bookmark important words directly from the word explanation popup
4. Access your bookmarked words anytime via the Bookmarks button
5. Search, sort, and manage your bookmarked vocabulary collection
6. Select passages for comprehensive text explanations and summaries
7. Click "Ask about page" for instant AI-powered summaries of entire webpages
8. Chat with AI in your preferred language for any questions
9. Learn and understand content more effectively, regardless of the website's language
